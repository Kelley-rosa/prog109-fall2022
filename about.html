About Me

Where are you from?

I was born and raised in San Antonio, Texas. In the fall of 2021, I moved to Redmond, Washington.

In how many languages can you say "hello"?

Four. Hello (English), Hola (Spanish), Kon'nichiwa (Japanese), and Hallo (German).
But I'm only fluent in English. I, however, may or may not be able to understand you if you're talking to me in Spanish.

What are you academic and professional goals?

My dream is graduate with a Bachelors in Computer Science. From there I'd want to have small jobs in big companies.
I rather do this so I can make money doing what I enjoy but not have too much responsibility that I can't enjoy life.

Who would you most like to meet in real life?

I would most like to meet Ludwig van Beethoven. I would love to hear his music either played or directed by him.
Also, I'd like to know what his personality is really like.

What’s the last thing that made you laugh out loud?

For backstory, I recently visited cousin that I had not seen in a long time. We played air hockey.
As time went on, the game became silly in the sense that no one could score.
No matter how close the puck came to the goal, something ridicules would happen and the score remained as was.

Where would you most like to go on vacation?

I would most like to go to Panama to fulfill a desire a friend of mine had before his passing.

What’s your earliest memory?

My earliest memory is almost drowning in a pool. Emphasis on the "almost". I remember choosing to jump in the deep end.
I was immediately pulled out of the water by my dad. Everyone was looking at me worried and I didn’t understand why.

What is the best gift you have ever received?

A puppy I named Copper (from Fox and the Hound). He was a surprise gift from my parents.

If you had a time machine, would you travel to the past of the future?

The past. I'd go back to see the precious things I missed from trying to get to the future too quickly.
